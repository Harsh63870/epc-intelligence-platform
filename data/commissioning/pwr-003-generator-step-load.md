# Commissioning Test: Generator Step Load
**Standard:** TIA-942  
**System:** Power  
**Procedure ID:** PWR-003

## Procedure
1. Start generator and stabilize at no load.
2. Apply 50%, 75%, and 100% step loads sequentially.
3. Record voltage and frequency deviation for each step.

## Acceptance Criteria
Voltage dip within **15%** during step load. Frequency recovery within 5 seconds.

## Records Required
Load bank report, governor response log, protective relay event log.
