# Commissioning Test: Chiller Failover
**Standard:** Uptime Tier III  
**System:** Cooling  
**Procedure ID:** CLG-001

## Procedure
1. Operate chiller plant at design load with N+2 configuration.
2. Trip two running chiller units simultaneously.
3. Monitor supply air temperature in adjacent data hall whitespace.

## Acceptance Criteria
Supply air temperature within **2°C of setpoint** within **10 minutes** of failover event.

## Records Required
BMS trend logs, chiller sequence of operation verification, witness checklist.
