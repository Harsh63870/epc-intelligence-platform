# Commissioning Test: Integrated System — Utility Loss
**Standard:** TIA-942  
**System:** Power  
**Procedure ID:** PWR-004

## Procedure
1. Operate facility at 50% design load with utility and generators on standby.
2. Simulate complete utility loss.
3. Verify automatic transfer to generator and UPS support without IT load interruption.

## Acceptance Criteria
**Zero IT load interruption** during planned transfer sequence. All sequences per approved SOO document SOO-E-001.

## Records Required
Integrated test script sign-offs, sequence timing log, incident-free certification letter.
