# Commissioning Test: CRAC Redundancy Failover
**Standard:** TIA-942  
**System:** Cooling  
**Procedure ID:** CLG-002

## Procedure
1. Operate data hall at design IT load.
2. Disable one CRAC unit in each redundant pair.
3. Monitor rack inlet temperatures for 60 minutes.

## Acceptance Criteria
No hot spot above **27°C** in whitespace during failover. All rack inlet temps within ASHRAE A1 envelope.

## Records Required
Wireless sensor log, BMS alarm history, thermal imaging report (spot check).
