# Commissioning Test: UPS Battery Discharge
**Standard:** Uptime Tier III  
**System:** Power  
**Procedure ID:** PWR-002

## Procedure
1. Disconnect utility feed to UPS module under test.
2. Apply full rated load (500 kVA) using load bank.
3. Record time to battery depletion alarm and shutdown threshold.

## Acceptance Criteria
Minimum **15 minutes runtime** at full load. Battery voltage within manufacturer curve at 15-minute mark.

## Records Required
Load bank data log, battery cell voltages, thermal readings.
