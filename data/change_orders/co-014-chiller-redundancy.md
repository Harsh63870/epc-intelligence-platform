# Change Order CO-014: Chiller Plant Redundancy Upgrade
**CO Number:** CO-014  
**Date:** 2025-05-20  
**Status:** Approved

## Description
Upgrade water-cooled chiller plant from N+1 to **N+2 redundancy** to align with Tier III certification path.

## Scope
- Add one (1) additional 2000 TR centrifugal chiller unit
- Revise controls sequence for N+2 failover
- Extend piping and electrical feeders to new chiller bay

## Cost / Schedule Impact
Cost: +₹4.2 Cr | Schedule: +3 weeks to procurement lead time
