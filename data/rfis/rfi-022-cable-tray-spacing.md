# RFI-022: Cable Tray Spacing — Electrical Room
**Status:** Answered  
**Discipline:** Electrical  
**Date raised:** 2025-04-02

## Question
What is the approved cable tray spacing in the main electrical room between power and control trays?

## Response
Minimum **300 mm vertical spacing** between power and control cable trays per NFPA 70 and drawing E-102. Maintain separation per manufacturer bend radius requirements.
