# RFI-014: UPS Battery Runtime Clarification
**Status:** Answered  
**Discipline:** Electrical  
**Date raised:** 2025-03-12

## Question
Confirm required UPS battery runtime at full load for Tier III certification for 500 kVA modules in Building A.

## Response
Minimum **15 minutes at full load** per Uptime Tier III and specification section 26 33 16. Battery sizing shall use EOL factor 0.85. Witness discharge test required prior to PAC.
