# RFI-051: STS Transfer Time Acceptance
**Status:** Answered  
**Discipline:** Electrical  
**Date raised:** 2025-06-01

## Question
What is the acceptance criteria for static transfer switch (STS) transfer time during integrated system testing?

## Response
Transfer time shall **not exceed 4 ms** for Class 1 STS per Uptime Institute guidelines. Witness test required with oscilloscope capture at site.
