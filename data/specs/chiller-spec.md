# Water-Cooled Chiller Plant Specification
**Project:** Mumbai Hyperscale DC-01  
**Section:** 23 64 00  
**Standard:** Uptime Tier III

## 1. Configuration
Water-cooled centrifugal chiller plant serving data hall CRAC units and central plant.

## 2. Redundancy
Cooling plant shall be configured **N+2 redundancy** for Tier III compliance. Loss of any two chiller units shall not cause supply air temperature to exceed design setpoint by more than 2°C for more than 10 minutes.

## 3. Capacity
Total installed cooling capacity: 12 MW. Each chiller unit minimum 2000 TR.

## 4. Efficiency
Minimum IPLV of 0.45 kW/TR per AHRI 550/590 test conditions.

## 5. Controls
Integration with BMS required. Remote monitoring of supply/return temperatures, flow rates, and chiller status.
