# Standby Diesel Generator Specification
**Project:** Mumbai Hyperscale DC-01  
**Section:** 26 32 13

## 1. Rating
Standby rated generator set minimum **4000 kW** at 0.8 power factor, 50 Hz, 11 kV output.

## 2. Fuel Storage
On-site diesel storage minimum **72 hours** at N-1 generator operation at full load.

## 3. Starting
Automatic start and load transfer within 10 seconds of utility failure signal.

## 4. Emissions
Compliant with CPCB norms for stationary diesel generators in Maharashtra.

## 5. Testing
Load bank test at 50%, 75%, and 100% load. Step load test per IEEE 1159 guidelines.
