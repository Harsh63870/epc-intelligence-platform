# Medium Voltage Switchgear Specification
**Project:** Mumbai Hyperscale DC-01  
**Section:** 26 13 00

## 1. Standard
Medium voltage switchgear shall comply with **UL 891**. IEC 61439 submittals are **not acceptable** for this project.

## 2. Rating
- Voltage class: 15 kV
- Continuous current: 2000 A minimum
- Short-circuit rating: 40 kA at 15 kV

## 3. Construction
Metal-clad, draw-out type circuit breakers. Arc-resistant design per IEEE C37.20.7 where applicable.

## 4. Protective Relaying
Microprocessor-based relays with event recording. Settings to be submitted for engineer review.

## 5. Testing
Factory acceptance test (FAT) and site acceptance test (SAT) required. Megger and contact resistance tests per NETA ATS.
